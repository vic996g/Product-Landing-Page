# Product Landing Page

A modern, responsive, and visually appealing **product landing page** built with **HTML and CSS**.  
Designed to showcase a product, highlight features, display testimonials, and present pricing in a professional style.

---

## 🔗 Live Demo

[View Live Demo](https://vic996g.github.io/Product-Landing-Page/)



---
## 💻 Technologies Used

- **HTML5** – Semantic markup for structure  
- **CSS3** – Styling, animations, and responsive design   
- **CSS Flexbox** – Layouts for hero, features, product gallery, and pricing  
- **CSS Animations & Transitions** – Hero image slider, testimonial slider, hover effects  

---

## 🖼 Sections

1. **Navbar** – Sticky, responsive navigation bar  
2. **Hero Section** – Hero image slider with animated transitions  
3. **Features** – Interactive feature cards with hover effects  
4. **Product Gallery** – Product showcase with hover zoom and lift effect  
5. **Testimonials** – Sliding customer testimonials  
6. **Pricing** – Pricing cards with featured highlights  
7. **CTA** – Call-to-action section with gradient background  
8. **Footer** – Simple footer with branding  

---

## 🎨 Design & UX Highlights

- Modern, clean design with **gradient backgrounds**  
- Fully **responsive layout** for desktop, tablet, and mobile  
- Smooth **hover effects** on features, products, and pricing cards  
- **Animated hero slider** with scale and opacity transitions  
- **Testimonial slider** showcasing customer reviews  

---

## ⚡ Features

- **Responsive Design** – Works on all screen sizes  
- **Interactive Hover Effects** – Cards and images lift and zoom on hover  
- **Smooth Animations** – Hero and testimonial sliders  
- **Reusable Components** – Easily add more features, products, or testimonials
